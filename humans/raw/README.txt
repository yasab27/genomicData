Files Enclosed:

"humanKOAbundances.xlsx": An excel document containing the abundance data measured from 1267 individuals from four different
countries for ~6000 genes. All genes are named according to their Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) Ortholog
number or KO Number.

NOTE: Entries [0,139) are American samples, [139, 507) are Chinese samples, [507, 908) are Danish Samples, and [909, 1267)
are from Spain. 

"humanModules.html": A sparse HTML document from the KEGG Mapper output representing the classification of the genes into 
the kegg standard reference modules. 

"humanPathways.html": A sparse HTML document from the KEGG Mapper output representing the classification of the genes into 
the kegg standard reference pathways.
